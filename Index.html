A memoir of the band Her's
This page is a fan made page dedicated to the British Indie band known as Her's. The artists of the band were Stephen Fitzpatrick (Guitar and vocals) and Audun Laading (Bass guitar). Their music will forever remain in the hearts of their fans and those who discover them years after their untimely passing.
Below are the albums that were released by Her's
Songs of Her's was the debut album for the band that was a compilation album for the Indie Pop band Her's. The album was released on May 12th, 2017 featuring songs hit songs Cool with you and What Once Was
Invitation to Her's was the one and only studio album produced by Her's which was released on August 24th, 2018. It featured hits such as Harvey and She Needs Him
